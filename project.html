PROJECT HARDWARE

Details of the electronic components and 3D printing used in the project.

Arduino Mega 2560

What is it? The Arduino Mega 2560 is a microcontroller board based on the ATmega2560. It has 54 digital input/output pins, 16 analog inputs, and ample memory for complex projects.

Purpose: Acts as the brain of the robotic arm, controlling all connected components such as servos, motors, and sensors.

Specifications:

  • Microcontroller: ATmega2560
  • Operating Voltage: 5V
  • Input Voltage: 7-12V (recommended)
  • Flash Memory: 256 KB
  • SRAM: 8 KB
  • EEPROM: 4 KB

HC-05 Bluetooth Module

What is it? The HC-05 is a Bluetooth module that enables wireless communication between the Arduino and other devices, such as a smartphone or computer.

Purpose: Allows remote control of the robotic arm via Bluetooth, enabling wireless operation and data exchange.

Specifications:

  • Bluetooth Version: 2.0 + EDR
  • Operating Voltage: 3.3V - 5V
  • Communication Range: Up to 10 meters
  • Interface: UART (Serial Communication)

MG996R Servo Motors (x5)

What is it? The MG996R is a high-torque servo motor commonly used in robotics for precise angular control.

Purpose: Used to control the joints of the robotic arm, providing accurate movement and positioning.

Specifications:

  • Operating Voltage: 4.8V - 7.2V
  • Stall Torque: 10 kg/cm (at 4.8V), 12 kg/cm (at 6.6V)
  • Speed: 0.17s/60° (at 4.8V), 0.14s/60° (at 6.6V)
  • Rotation Angle: 180°

NEMA 17 Stepper Motor (34mm Height)

What is it? The NEMA 17 is a stepper motor commonly used in precision motion control applications.

Purpose: Provides precise rotational control for specific parts of the robotic arm, such as the base or gripper.

Specifications:

  • Step Angle: 1.8° (200 steps per revolution)
  • Holding Torque: 40-50 Ncm
  • Operating Voltage: 12V - 24V
  • Current: 1.2A - 1.7A per phase

Additional Information

3D Printing Details

  • Filament Type: PLA (Polylactic Acid) and PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol)
  • Filament Color: White
  • Printer Model: FlashForge AD5M
  • Layer Height: 0.2mm
  • Infill Percentage: 20%
  • Supports: Used for overhangs; removed with TLC after printing.
  • Slicer: Orca-FlashForge
