About Me

Hi! My name is George Velenzas, and I'm a Fiber Optics Technician with a passion for learning and building. This website, The Noob’s Guide to a Robotic Arm, is where I document my journey of building a robotic arm from scratch. I’m no expert—just a curious noob who loves to build things!

With a background in optical networks and a hands-on approach to problem-solving, I thrive in environments that challenge me to innovate and learn. This project is my love for building and coding and I’m excited to share my progress and my mistakes with you!

About The Project

Building this robotic arm has been my first adventure in robotics, and it's been an incredible (and sometimes frustrating) learning experience. The goal is to build a fully functional robotic arm controlled wirelessly via a mobile app. It’s a work in progress, and I’m learning as I go—so don’t expect perfection!
